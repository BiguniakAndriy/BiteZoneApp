# Privacy Policy for BiteZone application

**App Name:** BiteZone  
**Developer:** Andriy Biguniak  
**Support Email:** biguniakdev@gmail.com  

---

## 1. Information We Collect
BiteZone does **not collect personal information** such as email or login credentials.  
The app stores **local data created by the user**, including notes, photos, and fishing session details. Users can optionally save **geolocation data** for fishing locations.

No analytics, tracking, or cookies are used.  

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data
All data is stored locally on the user’s device and is used exclusively for:  
- Reviewing past fishing sessions  
- Analyzing data to provide suggestions for future fishing locations  
- Enhancing the user’s personal experience within the app  

---

## 3. Payment Information
Any payments for subscriptions or in-app purchases are processed **exclusively through Apple’s payment system**. BiteZone does not store or access credit card information directly.

---

## 4. Data Sharing
BiteZone **does not share user data with third parties**. All information remains on the user’s device unless the user explicitly exports it.

---

## 5. Age of Users
BiteZone is designed for **all ages**. There are no restrictions based on age.

---

## 6. User Rights
Users have full control over their data:  
- They can **view, modify, or delete** their stored data  
- Since data is stored locally, users can also **opt out** of using features that store information  

---

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Users will be informed via app updates.фзз
